Panel Of Delegates

Pooja Makhija

Nutritionist

She is the nutritionist to stars like Deepika Padukone, Karan Johar, Ranbir Kapoor and Shahid Kapoor, functional & lifestyle practitioner and TedEx speaker, Pooja Makhija has, to date, transformed the bodies – and the lives – of thousands of people. Her bestselling book released in 2012, Eat Delete - has been translated into Hindi, Bengali, Gujarati and Marathi. In 2017 she launched her second book – Eat Delete Junior, a parental read on child nutrition and recently launched N for Nourish, India's first children’s book aimed at teaching kids about nutrition. She also had her own TV show – the Pooja Makhija show – on the Food Food channel, her own radio show on Magic FM , and has a regular column in the Times of India.

Ishi Khosla

Clinical Nutritionist and Author

Ishi Khosla is a renowned clinical nutritionist, consultant, author, and entrepreneur. Based in New Delhi, she runs the Centre for Dietary Counseling and founded the pioneering health food company 'Whole Foods'. Ishi has penned several books, including 'The Diet Doctor', 'Is Wheat Killing You?', 'Eating At Work' and 'The 4G Code to Good Health'. Her expertise extends to television, where she is a regular commentator, and she has been honored as one of India's twenty-five most powerful women by the India Today Group. She also serves on several prestigious committees and is the founder of The Celiac Society of India. She has also founded a wellness app Welmo to be launched shortly.

Tanvi Gulati

Nutritionist, Eatsmart

She has done master's in food & nutrition (MSc.) with vast experience in Therapeutic diet and disease management. She is running an Online Dietitian Clinic ' Eat Smart with Dt Tanvi' since last eight years, wherein she has transformed lives of a thousand clients across the globe.- Ex- Clinical Nutritionist at Max Hospital, Mohali & Bathinda wherein she worked in Hospital to manage the Diet planning for the patients.- Ex Clinical Nutritionist with Abott India wherin she worked with leading Doctors in Patiala on Gaestro and Diabetes therapeutic diet management.

Ritika Sammadar

Chief Dietitian, Max Healthcare

Ritika Samaddar is a renowned nutritionist with extensive experience of 30 yrs in clinical nutrition and dietetics. She has been the Regional Head of Clinical Nutrition and Dietetics at Max Healthcare since 2005. Ritika holds a Master's degree in Therapeutic Nutrition from Lady Irwin College and is a trained Bariatric Nutritionist from Hermann Memorial Hospital, USA and a qualified sports Nutritionist. She is a Joint Secretary of the Delhi Chapter of the Indian Dietetic Association and has received multiple awards for her contributions to the field of dietetics.

Namratha Pramod

Certified Sports Nutritionist, Performance Analyst at SAI

She brings over three decades of expertise in clinical nutrition, nutrition research, and academia. Her expertise lies in providing nutritional guidance and counselling to people of all ages, addressing health concerns such as obesity, diabetes, and other chronic medical conditions.

Anita Jatana

Chief Dietician – Indraprastha Apollo Hospital Delhi

She was the recipient of the Scroll of Honor, awarded for ‘professional achievement in Lady Irwin College’ for her outstanding contribution in the field of Dietetics and the Lifetime Achievement Award given by the Lady Irwin College Alumni Association for her dedicated contributions in the field of dietetics in 2023. She has also presented more than a hundred scientific papers at various platforms, contributed chapters to multiple books and has edited the book-Apollo Clinical Nutrition Handbook.
